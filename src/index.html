Dr. Mustofa Mahmud Al Mamun

I am a Macroeconomist specializing in Macroeconomic Policy. I use a mix of econometric and general equilibrium models to answer important policy questions. My recent research focused on the short-run effects of unanticipated changes in government spending.

I completed my PhD in Economics at Fordham University in 2024. Prior to my Doctoral Studies, I earned a Master’s degree in Economics from the University of Arizona and a Bachelor’s degree from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET).
